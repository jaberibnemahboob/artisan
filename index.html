About the Glass Artist

Myself

My main interest in glassblowing is to create timeless design and iconic form of visual language. I have excelled in many forms and styles which include the use of clean lines and superior craftsmanship. Using my past experiences I spent a lot of time developing my own colors that transform into one of a kind works of art.
